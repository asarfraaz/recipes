Ingredients:
  - 1 egg
  - 1 teaspoon oil
  - a pinch of salt

Directions :
  1. Heat a pan
  2. Pour a teaspoon of oil in it and wait till it heats up
  3. Crack open the egg and directly pour its contents onto the oil carefully
  4. Wait for the egg to turn browish at the bottom
  5. Add a pinch of salt to taste
